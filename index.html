♞ UNIVERSAL REVERSI

HOW TO PLAY
Objective
Have the most pieces when neither player can move.

Placing
Click a highlighted cell to place your piece. You must flank at least one opponent piece in any direction.

Flanking
All opponent pieces between your new piece and another of yours (in a straight line) flip to your colour.

Toroidal board
Opposite edges are connected — lines can wrap around. Use ↑↓←→ or the arrow buttons to pan the view.

Pass
If you have no valid move, your turn is skipped.

Board size
Change size & press Restart Game.
